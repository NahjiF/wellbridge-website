Welcome to WellBridge Social

WellBridge Social is a locally owned event and conference venue in Birmingham, AL, operated by Lynn Wilkerson. Our goal is to make hosting your event simple, affordable, and stress-free.

With two flexible spaces, included tables and chairs, and optional linen rentals, you can shape the room to match your colors, theme, and style. Bring your own planner or work directly with Lynn to create the experience you’re imagining.

FAQ's

How do I reserve a date?

A signed rental agreement and a deposit of half your total balance are required to hold your date. The remaining balance is due at least 5 calendar days before your event.

Is there a cleaning fee?

A $100 clean-up deposit is collected before your event and returned promptly once the room is left clean. Floors and tables must be clear of debris, and all trash placed in the provided cans.

Can I bring my own decorations?

Yes. You're welcome to decorate the space to match your theme. Please plan your setup and breakdown within your rental time.
