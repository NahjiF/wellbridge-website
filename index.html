Welcome to WellBridge Social

WellBridge Social is a locally owned event and conference venue in Birmingham, AL, operated by Lynn Wilkerson. Our goal is to make hosting your event simple, affordable, and stress-free.

With two flexible spaces, included tables and chairs, and optional linen rentals, you can style the room to match your colors and theme. Bring your own planner, caterer, DJ, and décor—or keep it simple and intimate.

Tables, Chairs & Linen

Included with all rentals:

  • 60" round tables
  • 6 ft tables
  • Folding chairs

Linen rentals:

  • Polyester tablecloths – $8 each
  • Chair covers & sashes – $1 each
  • Overlays – $4 each
  • Runners – $3 each
  • Specialty cloths – $15 each

We set up and take down all rented linen for you.

Clean Up Deposit

A refundable $100 clean up deposit is required before your event. It will be returned if:

  • Floors and tables are free of debris
  • Trash is bagged and placed in provided cans
  • Decorations are removed and broken down
  • Breakdown is completed within your rental time

If not, the deposit will be forfeited.

Alcohol & Security

If you have more than 50 guests and serve alcohol (BYOB or open bar), the City of Adamsville requires a police officer on site.

To arrange and pay for an officer, please contact the City of Adamsville at 205-674-5671.
