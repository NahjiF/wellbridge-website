Welcome to WellBridge Social

WellBridge Social is a locally owned event and conference venue in Birmingham, AL, operated by Lynn Wilkerson. Our goal is to make hosting your event simple, affordable, and stress-free.

With two flexible spaces, included tables and chairs, and optional linen rentals, you can style the room to match your colors and theme. Bring your own planner, caterer, DJ, and décor—or keep it simple and intimate.

2026 Rental Information

Clear pricing for both the large and small rooms. Pricing is based on room size, event type, day of the week, time needed, number of guests, linen rental, and whether alcohol will be served.

All rentals are for up to 8 hours. Additional time is $100 per hour. Events must end on time; setup and cleanup are included in your 8-hour window.

Clean Up Deposit

A refundable $100 clean up deposit is required before your event. It will be returned if the venue is left clean and in good condition.

Tables, Chairs & Linen

Tables and chairs are included with all rentals. Linen and specialty cloths are available for an additional fee.

Alcohol & Security

If you have more than 50 guests and serve alcohol (BYOB or open bar), the City of Adamsville requires a police officer on site. To arrange and pay for an officer, please contact the City of Adamsville at 205-674-5671.

FAQ's

Answers to a few common questions about renting the venue.

Can I bring my own food and cake?

Yes. You’re welcome to bring your own food, cake, and non-alcoholic drinks, or use the caterer of your choice.

Can I bring a DJ and loud music?

Yes, DJs are allowed. We just ask that music volume remains respectful of our neighbors.

Do you offer tours?

Absolutely. Contact us to schedule a tour and see the space in person.

Do you provide décor?

We do not provide full décor services, but we can recommend decorators and planners who know the space well.
