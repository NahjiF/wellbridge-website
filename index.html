Frequently Asked Questions

Can we serve alcohol?

Yes—if you have more than 50 guests and serve alcohol, the City of Adamsville requires a police officer.

Is setup time included?

Yes, setup and breakdown time are included in your 8-hour rental window.

How do I reserve a date?

A signed contract plus 50% deposit holds your date. Remaining balance due 5 days before event.
