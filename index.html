Welcome to WellBridge Social

WellBridge Social is a locally owned event and conference venue in Birmingham, AL, operated by Lynn Wilkerson. Our goal is to make hosting your event simple, affordable, and stress-free.

With two flexible spaces, included tables and chairs, and optional linen rentals, you can shape the room to match your colors, theme, and style.

Large Room

Small Room

Ideal for up to 50 guests.

  • Day events (9am–5pm): $600
  • Evening events (4pm–12am / 5pm–1am / 6pm–2am): $700
  • Includes tables & chairs

Linen & Extras

  • Polyester tablecloths: $8 each
  • Chair covers & sashes: $1 each
  • Overlays: $4 each
  • Specialty cloths (sequin/rosette): $15 each

We set up and break down all rented linen for you.

All rental prices are for up to 8 hours. Additional time is $100 per hour. Cleanup deposit: $100 (refunded when the room is left clean and trash removed).

How It Started

WellBridge Social was created to give families in the Birmingham / Forestdale area a beautiful, flexible space for life’s celebrations — without the stress and hidden fees of larger venues.
